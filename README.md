# kiriVid
Stunning video capture tool with audio. Also has After Effects-like video editor

[Download KiriVid](https://beta-japan.com/exis/?p=kirivid&t=1)

---

A video-capture tool that allows you to record your desktop with audio. You can also edit recordings on the spot, and it comes with a highly advanced video editor similar to After Effects.

It runs extremely fast and has a download size of only about 3MB (Windows version).


This app supports the following features:

🟩 Easily record your desktop screen with audio

🟩 Record from webcam and microphone

🟩 Export to .mp4/.mp3/.webm/.gif/.webp/.apng

🟩 Supports dual monitors: record a specific screen or all displays simultaneously

🟩 Supports 4K and even 8K video output (with upscaling functionality)

🟩 Crop and edit recorded footage

🟩 Advanced video-editing features similar to After Effects

🟩 Supports 3D images and 3D text, with camera and keyframe control

🟩 Includes a variety of built-in animation templates

🟩 Overlay GIF animations onto videos

🟩 Convert videos to .mp3 with customizable audio quality

🟩 Supports GIF and WEBP export

🟩 Supports importing 3D models (.glb) to overlay over recorded videos

🟩 Supports animations and motion for imported 3D models

🟩 Adjustable FPS output

🟩 Save project files and presets
