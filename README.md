## Overview
kiriVid is a lightweight video capture tool that records your desktop **with system audio**, and lets you **edit immediately** after recording.  
It also includes an advanced editor with motion graphics features inspired by After Effects.

> **Size:** 3MB (Windows)  
> **Performance:** optimized for ultra fast capture & editing

## Features

### Capture
- ✅ Record your desktop screen with audio
- ✅ Record from webcam and microphone
- ✅ Dual monitor support (record one display or all)

### Export
- ✅ Export to `mp4` / `mp3` / `webm` / `gif` / `webp` / `apng`
- ✅ Adjustable FPS output
- ✅ Convert video to `mp3` with customizable audio quality
- ✅ GIF & WEBP export support

### Editing
- ✅ Crop and edit recorded footage
- ✅ After Effects‑like advanced editing tools
- ✅ Built‑in animation templates
- ✅ Overlay GIF animations onto videos
- ✅ Save project files and presets

### 3D
- ✅ 3D images and 3D text (camera + keyframes)
- ✅ Import 3D models (`.glb`) and overlay them on videos
- ✅ Animation / motion controls for imported 3D models

### High resolution
- ✅ Supports 4K and up to 8K output (with upscaling)
